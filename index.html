Our Founder

A Family Business

Coco’s Chocolatiers, contrary to popular belief, was not built by Coco herself, but rather her great-great-grandson, who used her recipes to start the business in her honour. What started as a simple operation out of the family home has grown to welcome customers and fans from across the country.

History

Since 1942

Since 1942, Coco’s Chocolatiers have been working their hardest to create the finest assortment of products, in order to honour the family matriarch, Coco. The name was an obvious choice, as Coco created all of the recipes herself and also shares her name with the richly flavourful beans that make up all our chocolate treats.
